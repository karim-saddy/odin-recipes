Margherita Pizza

Margherita pizza is a simple yet flavorful Italian dish made with a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil. Its minimal ingredients highlight the fresh, authentic flavors of traditional pizza.

Perfect for a quick homemade meal, Margherita pizza is best enjoyed fresh out of the oven with a drizzle of olive oil for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread an even layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Place slices of fresh mozzarella on top.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  6. Bake for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Top with fresh basil leaves after baking.
  8. Slice and serve hot.
