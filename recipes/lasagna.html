Lasagna

Lasagna is a hearty, oven-baked dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese. Its flavors deepen as it bakes, making it a comforting and satisfying meal.

Perfect for family dinners or meal prep, lasagna can be customized with different fillings like vegetables or ricotta. Serve it hot with a side salad and garlic bread for a classic Italian experience.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets (12 sheets)
  • Ground beef (1 lb / 450g)
  • Tomato sauce (28 oz / 800g)
  • Onion (1 small, chopped)
  • Garlic (3 cloves, minced)
  • Ricotta cheese (15 oz / 425g)
  • Mozzarella cheese (2 cups, shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (½ cup, grated)
  • Egg (1, for ricotta mixture)
  • Dried Italian herbs (1 tsp basil & oregano mix)

Steps

  1. Cook the onion, garlic, and ground beef in a pan until browned.
  2. Add the tomato sauce and herbs, then let it simmer.
  3. Mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan in a bowl.
  4. Boil the lasagna noodles, then drain and set aside.
  5. Spread a layer of sauce in a baking dish, then add noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  6. Repeat the layers and finish with sauce and cheese on top.
  7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 40 minutes until bubbly.
  8. Let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
